Breadcrumbs is a website navigational aid, usually near the
top of a page, showing the path you have followed to reach
a given page, or where the page you are viewing resides in
a website.

Search Engine is a mechanism for finding content on the
Internet or on a website. The most familiar Internet search
engine is google.com.

HTTP is the standard for managing communications
between web browsers and web servers.

IP address is a unique numerical address assigned to every
device and resource on the Internet and similar networks.
